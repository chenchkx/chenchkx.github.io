About Me
I am currently a Ph.D. student in the College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Mingli Song. Before 2020, I received my M.S. degree from Jiangnan University in June 2019, supervised by Prof. Xiao-Jun Wu and co-supervised by Prof. Josef Kittler. I received my B.E. degree from the Jiangsu University of Science and Technology in June 2016. From 2019 to 2020, I worked as an image processing engineer at a leading technology company located in Hangzhou, China. More details can be found here.

Research Interests:

  • Computer Vision:
    • image set/video representation learning, e.g., Riemannian encoder, etc.
    • manifold/metric learning with application to image set/video classification, etc.
    • decentralized learning, etc.
  • Graph Mining:
    • graph neural network, graph pooling, graph normalization, graph lottery tickets, etc.
    • graph representation learning in power system analysis, application, etc.
